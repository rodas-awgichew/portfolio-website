About

UI/UX Designer & Web Developer.

I'm Rodas Awgichew — a front-end developer with a passion for crafting responsive and visually engaging web experiences. I specialize in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Tailwind CSS, blending clean code with elegant design to build user-friendly interfaces that not only look great but function flawlessly. My approach is grounded in accessibility, performance, and aesthetic precision. Whether it's animated hero sections, luxury-themed layouts, or scroll-triggered micro-interactions, I bring creative energy and attention to detail to every project. Let's build something beautiful — one pixel at a time.
